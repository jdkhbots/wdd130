Choose your Adventure

Thrill and Drill

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush as you navigate through rapids in this heart-pounding adventure. Perfect for those seeking a high-octane thrill in the wildest parts of the Volta river. It is made for a full day and suitable for experts or those willing to become experts. This adventure is best for Spring and Summer but works fine for other seasons as well. Try this adventure, you won't regret it.

Family Fun Float

A gentle ride ideal for families with young children. Enjoy the beautiful scenery and mild rapids while having a splash with your loved ones. Includes a picnic lunch by the riverbank. This trip takes you through the Nzulezu village. It is suitable for all; those without a rafting experience and those who are extremely good at it. To create beautiful memories with your family, this is your adventure.

Eco Explorer's Journey

A guided trip that focuses on the natural beauty and ecological significance of the river Prah. Learn about local wildlife and ecosystems while enjoying a moderately paced rafting adventure. This trip will take a full day and will take you through serveral towns along the river. Exploring is at the heart of this trip. You will also enjoy the view of the twin of the River Prah; River Ofin. Enjoy Nature with us!
